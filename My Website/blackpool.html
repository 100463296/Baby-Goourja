Blackpool Pleasure Beach

Down on the coast in sunny Blackpool, stands the pleasure beach. With its towering rides its sure to make any thrill seeker excited!

This park isn't only popular for its list of thrill rides but the history of the park that opened in 1896, it has lots of vintage rides that are still open to ride today.

In 2018, the park opened its latest and greatest coaster: Icon

This coaster launches rides twice at speeds up to 50 mph, making this the only multi launch coaster in the UK.

This ride twists and turns past all the other coasters around the park

Leaving riders wanting to go round again and again!
